01 — Capabilities

A control layer for your whole machine.

Ezriz sits one keystroke away. It sees your screen, talks to any model, and turns intent into actions on your PC.

/01

Every model, one shortcut.

Plug in your own Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, DeepSeek, Kimi and OpenRouter keys and switch between them mid-task. No app-hopping, no lock-in.

Multi-provider
/02

Screenshot analysis.

Capture any window or region and let the model read it — charts, errors, docs, UI. Ask about exactly what you're looking at.

Screen vision
/03

Actions, not just answers.

Chain prompts into real tasks: rename files, draft replies, run scripts, reshape data. Ezriz executes locally on your machine.

Automation
/04

Built for keyboard-first people.

A global hotkey summons the overlay over any app. Command palettes, saved prompts and per-app context, all driveable without the mouse.

Keyboard-native
/05

Your keys, your data.

Keys live under your account with row-level security and are fetched only by your signed-in client. No telemetry, no prompt logging.
